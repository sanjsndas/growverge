How we use cookies and similar technologies on our website

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the website owners. Cookies typically contain anonymous information such as a unique identifier, the site name, and some digits and numbers. They are used to remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience. Cookies can be categorized into different types, including session cookies, which are temporary and expire once you close your browser, and persistent cookies, which remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. Understanding cookies is essential for managing your privacy and security online.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use several types of cookies on our website to enhance user experience. These include essential cookies, which are necessary for the website to function correctly, performance cookies that help us understand how visitors interact with our site, and functionality cookies that allow us to remember your preferences. Additionally, we may use targeting cookies to deliver relevant advertisements to you based on your interests. Each type of cookie serves a specific purpose and contributes to the overall functionality of our website.

3. How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us improve our website by enabling us to analyze user behavior and preferences. By understanding how our website is used, we can make informed decisions about future improvements and updates.

4. Third-Party Cookies

5. Managing Cookies
